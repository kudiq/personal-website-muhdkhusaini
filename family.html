My Family

On the far left are my twin brother named Muhammad Khuzairi and my younger sister named Qhuratul Ain. I also have two younger brothers who are still in primary school and their names are Muhammad Khoirunnaas (flapping style) and Muhammad Hazrulkhoir (who does dab posture). My brother is a STPM graduate who is waiting for the UPU decision to leave and at the same time he works as a crew at the Japan Roads branch. My younger sister is preparing for the SPM exam after a year of delay due to the Covid-19 pandemic.
